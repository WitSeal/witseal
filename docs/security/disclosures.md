# Security disclosures & acknowledgements

This page is the public ledger of disclosed vulnerabilities in WitSeal, their
fixes, and (with consent) the reporters who found them. It is referenced by
[`SECURITY.md`](../../SECURITY.md) and [`SECURITY.txt`](../../SECURITY.txt).

## Status

**No disclosures yet — WitSeal is a pre-release (`v1.0` not reached).**

## How to report

Report privately via a GitHub security advisory:
<https://github.com/WitSeal/witseal/security/advisories/new>. Do **not** open a
public issue for a suspected vulnerability. See [`SECURITY.md`](../../SECURITY.md)
for what is in and out of the Phase 1 threat model.

## Process

- **Acknowledgement:** within 72 hours of a report.
- **Coordinated disclosure window:** 90 days from acknowledgement, or until a fix
  ships — whichever is sooner — adjustable by mutual agreement.
- **Fix + entry:** once a fix is released, an entry is added to the table below
  (advisory ID, affected versions, fix version, summary, reporter credit).
- **Credit:** reporters who request credit are listed with a link of their
  choice; reporters who prefer anonymity are credited as "anonymous researcher"
  or omitted, per their preference.
